## ‚ö†Ô∏è KNOWN LIMITATIONS

### **1. Extreme Cases (Outside Training Range)**
- **Very Short:** <5'4" (outside dataset range)
- **Very Tall:** >6'9" (outside dataset range)
- **Recommendation:** Display warning for heights outside 5'4" - 6'9"

### **2. Body Composition**
- Model doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat
- May over-predict for stocky builds (e.g., Kyle Schwarber +4.2 mph)
- May under-predict for lean builds at same weight
- **V2.0.3 Fix:** Add weight-to-height ratio adjustment

### **3. Hitting Style**
- Model assumes optimal/average technique
- Contact hitters may be slightly over-predicted (e.g., Xander Bogaerts +4.1 mph)
- Pure power hitters may be slightly under-predicted
- **V2.0.3 Fix:** Add motor profile interaction coefficients

### **4. Age Adjustment**
- Current age adjustment is simplified (linear after 35)
- Real aging curves are more complex
- **V2.0.3 Fix:** Implement more sophisticated age modeling

**Impact:** These limitations affect <5% of predictions (2/50 in validation) and are acceptable for V2.0.2.
