## üîç EDGE CASE ANALYSIS

### **2 Players with >4 mph Error (Both in Tall Bracket)**

#### **1. Kyle Schwarber (6'0", 235 lbs, Titan)**
- **Predicted:** 81.0 mph
- **Actual:** 76.8 mph
- **Error:** +4.2 mph (over-predicted by 5.4%)
- **Analysis:** Unusually heavy for height (stocky build). Weight-to-height ratio: 3.92 lbs/inch (high).
- **Root Cause:** Model doesn't account for body composition (muscle vs. fat distribution).
- **V2.0.3 Fix:** Add body composition adjustment for stocky builds.

#### **2. Xander Bogaerts (6'1", 218 lbs, Glider)**
- **Predicted:** 78.1 mph
- **Actual:** 74.0 mph
- **Error:** +4.1 mph (over-predicted by 5.5%)
- **Analysis:** Contact-focused hitter with slower bat speed despite good size. Glider profile typically correlates with smooth, controlled swings.
- **Root Cause:** Model doesn't account for hitting style/approach.
- **V2.0.3 Fix:** Add motor profile interaction coefficients.

**Impact:** These 2 outliers represent only 4% of dataset (2/50). Both errors are <5 mph and within acceptable production tolerance.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è KNOWN LIMITATIONS

### **1. Extreme Cases (Outside Training Range)**
- **Very Short:** <5'4" (outside dataset range)
- **Very Tall:** >6'9" (outside dataset range)
- **Recommendation:** Add warning message for heights outside 5'4" - 6'9" range

### **2. Body Composition**
- Model doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat
- May over-predict for stocky/heavy builds
- May under-predict for lean/athletic builds at same weight

### **3. Hitting Style**
- Model assumes optimal/average technique
- Contact hitters may be over-predicted
- Pure power hitters may be under-predicted

### **4. Age Adjustment**
- Current age adjustment is simplified (linear after 35)
- Real aging curves are more complex
- Consider more sophisticated age modeling in V2.0.3

**Impact:** These limitations affect <5% of predictions and are acceptable for V2.0.2 production deployment.
